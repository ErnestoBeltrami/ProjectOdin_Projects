Mozzarella in Carrozza Recipe


Description

A crispy fried sandwich with melted mozzarella inside â€” a classic Italian comfort food, perfect as a snack or appetizer.


Ingredients
  • 8 slices white bread (crust removed)
  • 200 g fresh mozzarella, sliced and drained
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Steps
  1. Place mozzarella slices between two bread slices; press edges gently.
  2. Whisk eggs, milk, salt, and pepper in a shallow bowl.
  3. Lightly coat sandwiches in flour.
  4. Dip in egg mixture, ensuring all sides are covered.
  5. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  6. Fry sandwiches until golden and crisp on both sides.
  7. Drain on paper towels; serve hot.
