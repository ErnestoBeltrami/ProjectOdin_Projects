Pancakes Recipe

Description

Soft, light, and perfect for breakfast or brunch. These pancakes are quick to prepare and delicious with syrup, fruit, or any topping you like.


Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps
  1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients; stir until just blended (donâ€™t overmix).
  4. Heat a lightly greased pan over medium heat.
  5. Pour 1/4 cup batter for each pancake; cook until bubbles form, then flip.
  6. Cook until golden; serve warm with toppings.
