Waffles Recipe


Description

Golden on the outside, soft inside â€” perfect for breakfast or a sweet treat. Serve with syrup, berries, or whipped cream.

Ingredients
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps
  1. Preheat waffle iron.
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, whisk eggs, milk, butter, and vanilla.
  4. Combine wet and dry ingredients; stir gently until just mixed.
  5. Pour butter into preheated iron; cook until golden and crisp.
  6. Serve hot with your favorite toppings.
